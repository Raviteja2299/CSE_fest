    About Us

    ABOUT THE PRESTO 2k22

    With the zeal of providing an outlook and a platform for the budding engineers across the Nation to create, explore learn and share various aspects, ALTS launches "PRESTO 2K22" A National Level Technical & Cultural Symposium  for two days. PRESTO aims to channelize together the leading research experts & professionals to share their knowledge and facilitate, transfer of information to budding engineers. The theme of the symposium is on "How to enhance the grip on technology and methods of explorations".

    ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

    Computer Science and Engineering is at the core of the information age. To prepare our students for the tremendous opportunities in the field, the CSE Department is strongly committed to excellence in both education and research. Our majors are designed to provide a strong foundation in the core areas of Computer Science and Engineering.
